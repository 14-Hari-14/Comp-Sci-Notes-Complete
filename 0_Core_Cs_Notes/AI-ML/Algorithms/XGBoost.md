## XGBoost
Its a short form for Extreme Gradient Boosting its a mathematical optimisation on top of normal gradient boosting, its very common in kaggle and other competitions for its accuracy and speed, it has another optimisation on top of it called LightGBM which is made by Microsoft which is even faster
