## CPU

- Describe the CPU architecture of a gameboy CPU
	- The gameboy used a chip called **Sharp LR35902** or **DMG-CPU**, its very similar to **intel 8080** and **zilog z80**. The chip follows the design principle of **SoC (System on Chip)**, where 1 chip houses and hides all components.
	- The gameboy CPU is an **8 bit CPU**. This means 1 register can hold 8 bits of data i.e 1 byte.
